## Trigger

- Dispatch success rate < 0.95
- Queue > 300 sec
- Matching rate < 0.90

## Cause

- Fleet shortage
- Node saturation
- ODD restriction
- Energy shortage

## Impact

- Waiting time increase
- ETA instability
- Fleet imbalance
- Service cancellation

## Recovery

- Reposition fleet
- Reduce operation area
- Increase ride sharing
- Restrict trip generation
